About Us

Our goal is to provide you with consistent quality at most competitive cost.

G-Line Glass is committed to sourcing only the very best quality glass products for ten years now by establishing relationships based on consistent sales volume and with some companies we have exclusivity for all sales into United States. With Rick being in Asia he visits each factory on a regular bases to inspect and ensure quality for our orders. While I am located in greater Seattle Wa area and worked for Hartung Glass ind for 18 years as corporate purchasing mgr I have an excellent understanding of our customers needs that I communicate to Rick in Asia.
